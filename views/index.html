THE ISSUE

Diarrhea, pneumonia, and other related diseases are major causes of child mortality in developing nations. According to UNICEF, on an annual basis more than 2.5 million children never see their 5th birthday because of these diseases. It is a direct result of poor hygienic conditions, little or no access to adequate sanitation, and a lack of health education among the youngest and most vulnerable. Asia and Africa alone account for 90% of the global child mortality.

THE OPPORTUNITY

On the other side of the coin, the hospitality industry is flushed with these hygienic amenities. We are talking about bars of soap, toothpaste, and more. However these are thrown out every single day. If recycled, we will divert potentially thousands of tonnes of waste from landfills each year. When well managed and with a little foresight this waste can be turned into lifesaving relief for millions of lives.

THE CATALYST

Today is a new age. Soaps4Lives partners with Hotels to protect the planet and save lives. We recycle slightly used soaps and other hygiene items and get them to disadvantaged or at risk communities. Our impact is to protect Mother Earth and save lives.

Partnership with Hotels

Soaps4Lives with hotels and other hospitality centers to save lives of millions across the globe. We collect and recycle slightly used soaps and other hygiene items that are then donated to disadvantaged or at risk communities to prevent child mortality. In so doing, we also help our partners increase their environmental responsibility.

Our Professional recycling process

Our recycling process controlled and sanitary risk free: it consists of sanitizing the discarded bars –removing any impurities, grounding them, and mixing the obtained soap noodles with water, to form new bars of soap. No chemical substance is added or used during the process. The obtained soap is then examined and tested to eliminate any medical risk.
